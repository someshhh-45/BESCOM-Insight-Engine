Problem framing

Challenges

  • Demand spikes are hard to predict locally.
  • Theft and tampering are not detected systematically.
  • Smart meter data remains underused.

Goals

  • Forecast hourly and day-ahead demand.
  • Classify zones by load risk.
  • Flag anomalous meters automatically.

Constraints

  • No change to existing systems.
  • Decision-support only, no auto action.
  • Explainable and auditable outputs.
